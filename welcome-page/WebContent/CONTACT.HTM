If you forget your Departmental Portal password or your password is expired, you can simply reset your password by your own.

Please follow the instructions below:-

(1) Click "Forget password" in log-in page

(2) Insert your user name e.g. tmchan

(3) Receive a Password Reset Token via email

(4) Type in the Token and set your new password in Password Reset Page

(5) Click "Submit"

If there is any problem in receiving the Token, you may apply for manual reset in writing to EO(Per)5 and include the following information:

- Full Name in English
- First 4 alphanumeric digits on HKID [A123***(*)]
- Clinic/Office
- Service
- Rank
- Contact Telephone Number
- Email Address

如果忘記密碼/密碼已經失效，你可以直接自行重設密碼。

請依從以下步驟重設密碼：

(1) 於登入頁面點選"Forget password"

(2) 輸入你的使用者名稱 e.g. tmchan

(3) 經電郵獲取驗証碼

(4) 於密碼重設頁面輸入驗証碼並更改密碼

(5) 點選 "Submit"

如無法獲取驗証碼，你仍可以書面向行政主任(人事)5申請手動重設密碼 ，並附上以下資料以供處理。

- 英文全名
- 身份證上首個英文字母及其後三個數目字[即A123***(*)]
- 診所/辦公室
- 服務單位
- 職級
- 聯絡電話
- 電郵地址
